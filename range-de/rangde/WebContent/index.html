About Rang De

We're a not-for-profit organization committed to fighting poverty by providing access to affordable micro loans to underserved communities in India. Join us.

Invest. In people. In potential. In pride.

1 out of 4 people in rural India live below the poverty line. Help change that statistic.
Experience Social Investing with Rang De.

Invest. In opportunity. In empowerment. In hands.


Experience Social Investing with Rang De.Invest.

  • Choose Borrower

    Rang De will select borrowers based on the preferences set by you.

  • Invest

    Enter the amount you want to invest in rural entrepreneurs or students.

  • Get Repaid

    The borrowers will return your loan with interest on monthly basis.

  • Making a social investment for the first time?
    Discover the joys of making a social investment and helping an individual overcome poverty.

  • Wonder what Rang De is all about?

    Rang De is a non-profit initiative that supports rural entrepreneurs with microloans for their business. Through our online platform, we help you support a specific entrepreneur with a social investment of Rs.100 or more!

  • Want to know more about social investing?

    Well, its just about helping someone overcome Poverty. In this case you also decide who you want to support and the individual you have supported repays your money with a small interest.

  • Worried about how much of your money will reach the individual you choose?

    We understand the value of your money and your desire to make a difference. We take great pride in the fact that 100% of your contribution goes to the individual you have chosen!

  • Concerned about the genuinity of the borrowers?

    Individuals listed on the website have been screened by our NGO field partners and then by our team before the loan is approved. So, you can rest assured that individuals have been screened.

  • Curious to know how you will choose an individual?

    You can search for individuals based on state or activity. Read through their story and invest Rs.100 or more.

  • If it's an investment, you will want to know how you will get the money back?

    As and when the individual repays the money, we will credit it into your Rang De account. You can reinvest this or withdraw it.

  • Anxious about entering your card details online?

    All our social investments take place over secure connections and your credit/debit card details are safe. Secure online transactions keep your bank and card provider in the loop as well

  • Want to get started right away?

    Thanks for making up your mind. Get started with your email id and a password.
